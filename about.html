About Me

My name is Eloise Ambursley. My motto, "Get Cultured," comes from my interest in diversity and how different cultures approach similar problems in different ways. I've spent some time volunteering abroad and have discovered how poverty can unlock creativity and cultivate resourcefulness, I've seen this in the people of the Philippines. My eyes have also opened to the opportunities hidden in history, this I've seen through my travels on the cobble streets of Europe. From these experiences, it has shaped the way I view the world and how I can interact with it through my own design solutions. I believe design is the consistent thread that flows through all cultures and binds us as one. Our approaches may be different but our purpose is one in the same which is, designing for solution and beauty. Thus, I've found the value in self-initiated projects. Most of my portfolio has come from being inspired by the world, addressing my own consumer pains, and creating possible solutions. I am proud to say that two of my projects are actually coming to life.

A very true statement is, "If you cook, they will come." I love food. I also love the culture surrounding food. Not just the different ingredients that create a delicious dish, but the power food has to bring people together, the power it has to stir a person's curiosity. Food is the simplest way to introduce one's culture/ethnicity. Food is the simplest way to welcome your new neighbour to the community. Like food, design also requires certain ingredients, certain tools, and a collaboration of people with different backgrounds, talents, skills, and of course, different and important experiences.

I enjoy a good meal, I enjoy the stories behind design, I enjoy the process and collaboration towards a solution. If you value the same things, connect with me and let's "cook" together. Nothing beats a great conversation around a table of good food.

I enjoy building a company’s brand and orchestrating an amazing end-user experience. I especially thrive in a team oriented environment with a purposeful culture.

Skilled in: UXUI, Branding, Front-End Development, Prototyping, Editorial, Typography, Videography, Photography, Packaging, Creative Writing, and Public Speaking.
