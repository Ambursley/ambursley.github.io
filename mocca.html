Playbook Planner Overview

  • Logo
  • Editorial

MOCCA is a Toronto contemporary arts museum. They’ve just hired a new CEO and are moving to a new location. She wanted a logo redesign for their main logo and a logo for their cafe. Her specific request is that the logo would not look like a “brand„ but more contemporary and I believe I've reached that specification. My role in this group project was to create a concept based on the idea of movement. After several ideation I came to this conclusion. I believe it represents art because this can be applied as a wire sculpture installation at their location. It is flexible, therefore it can be applied to their wayfinding system. I decided to stick to black because ultimately the idea is that it is so flexible any colour can be applied.
