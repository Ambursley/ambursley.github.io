Big Baller Brand Shoe Design Overview

  • Logo
  • Editorial

If you haven't heard of the Big Baller Brand, you need to YouTube the Ball brothers: LaMelo, LiAngelo, and Lonzo Ball. Watch these boys seriously ball. All boys are over 6 ft and have all committed to UCLA. The youngest is only 14 and has been committed to UCLA since he was 13. Lonzo, the eldest brother has just completed his first year at UCLA and is now entering the 2017 NBA draft. He will be the first NBA player to enter the draft with an established brand attached to his name. LaVar Ball, Lonzo's father, is not planning to sell out to big brands like Nike or Adidas, instead, he would prefer to collab with these companies for a price. I wanted to design Lonzo Ball a shoe that catered to his needs. Inspired by the Huaraches, this is my design.

Lonzo is 6 feet 8 inches. A big guy and who is full of surprises on and off the court. I really wanted the shoe to be an accurate interpretation of Lonzo's character. On the outside, Lonzo appears humble and kind-hearted, but on the court he is an assassin who will not stop at anything until he gets the win. He knows why basketball is played and that is to win. This shoe design is for winners. This shoe design is for Big Ballers like Lonzo. I designed high tops for him so that he can have ankle support. Nylon Cordura is breathable material to help air his feet. Carbon fibre is a lightweight material that is usually used on soccer player shoes. This material is applied around the ankle of the shoe for additional support. Shock absorbers are added to the soles to lessen the pressure on his knees after those high jumps and spectacular dunks. I chose a suede finish because all big ballers wear suede.

I designed three possible colour ways because we don't know where he will be drafted yet. I personally hope he goes to Miami because c’mon, look at that red shoe. It's beautiful.
