Mienne Perfume

  • Branding
  • Package Design
  • Advertising

Mienne is a fragrance designed for women, but not a typical “pretty„ and “elegant„ women but a woman who has the whole package. Mienne’s spokeswoman is Zoe Kravitz. Zoe is edgy, talented, and an individual who sets her own trends and is true to her being. That is what Mienne stands for, “mine,” individuality. The blooming flower because like Zoe Mienne is blooming and breaking out of the norm.

I designed the insert first and that is how I came up with the logo. I love to crest origami and that's how I came up the the origami flower insert.
